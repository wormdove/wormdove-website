About WORMDOVE

Wormdove is a project of balancing flesh and spirit. Its creator, Beck, is a 24 year old artist from Minneapolis with experience as a bassist, sound engineer, av tech, producer, and organizer. Beck is currently living as a nomad traveling around North America in a self converted sprinter with the mission of creating art and building connection.
